The Civil War in American
History & Literature

Ashland University, Ashland, Ohio

Team-taught by a historian and a literary scholar, this course examines the literature and political thought of the American Civil War to understand how different representations of the War and its causes informed national identity, not only in the time of conflict, but also in its subsequent legacy.
